# Premium Matcha Brand Director

Use this skill when designing copy, layout, sections, imagery, tone, and conversion flow for the matcha ecommerce site.

## Brand rules
- The site must feel calm, premium, natural, Japanese-inspired, and modern.
- Avoid cheap wellness clichés.
- Avoid generic green gradients, fake leaves everywhere, and overused "zen" copy.
- Copy must be short, sensory, and confident.
- Product must feel high quality, not mass-market.
- Animation should support the ritual story, not distract from buying.

## Visual direction
- Lots of whitespace.
- Deep matcha green, warm cream, soft stone, off-white, muted gold only if tasteful.
- Typography should feel editorial and premium.
- Use macro product imagery, powder texture, ceramic bowls, bamboo whisk, pouring motion.

## Conversion rules
- Every animation section must still explain one buying reason.
- Never hide the buy button too long.
- Mobile must be clean and fast.
- Product cards must be clear: name, use case, price, CTA.
